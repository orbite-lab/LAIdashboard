### BEPO (MedinCell)

**Posture:** selective  
**Open indications:** addiction, hiv, metabolic, pain  
**Closed indications:** psych, oncology, ophthalmology, cns_neuro, other  

BEPO is the second most-validated in-situ depot LAI platform after FluidCrystal. For BD scouts, BEPO is attractive if your molecule is a small molecule needing 1-3 month duration with strong IP runway (2031 core + continuations to 2038+). Important caveat: most TAs are encumbered. Open scope effectively limited to addiction (commercially crowded), HIV, non-incretin metabolic, pain. AbbVie collaboration locks oncology, ophthalmology, CNS-neuro, and "other"; Teva locks psych. New partner inquiries should focus on the unfilled HIV/metabolic/ pain band or wait for AbbVie program drops to free up space.

### FluidCrystal (Camurus)

**Posture:** selective  
**Open indications:** oncology, ophthalmology, cns_neuro, pain  
**Closed indications:** addiction, metabolic  

Most differentiated in-situ depot for peptides and broad duration range (weekly to six-monthly). For BD scouts, FluidCrystal is the platform of choice if your molecule is a peptide, requires cold-chain-free room-temp stability, or needs duration flexibility. Open indications for new partners: oncology, ophthalmology, CNS-neuro, pain. Closed: addiction (Braeburn), metabolic/endocrine incretins (Lilly), rare-endocrine setmelanotide (Rhythm). Two approved products (Buvidal, Brixadi) and Lund-based commercial CMC capacity reduce technical risk for new programs. Mid-teen royalty has been achieved historically (Braeburn 2014); tier-1 deals compress to mid-single-digit (Lilly 2025).
